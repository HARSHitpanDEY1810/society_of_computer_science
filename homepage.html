About The Society

The Computer Science Society of IERT Prayagraj is a student-driven initiative dedicated to fostering technical excellence and innovation. We aim to bridge the gap between academic curriculum and industry demands by organizing workshops, coding contests, and collaborative projects.

Read Our Constitution

Our Focus Areas

Empowering students through hands-on learning, collaborative projects, and competitive environments to build the engineers of tomorrow.

Competitive Coding

Regular contests and DSA sessions to sharpen problem-solving skills and prepare for technical interviews at top firms.

Open Source Collab

Fostering a culture of building together through open-source contributions, real-world projects, and team hackathons.

Collaborative Development

Encouraging teamwork in building real-world applications through shared coding, project-based learning, code reviews, and modern development workflows..
