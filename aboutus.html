About The Society

The Computer Science Society (CSS) at the Institute of Engineering and Rural Technology, Prayagraj, is a formal student-led body established to promote technical research, software development, and industrial preparedness within the Department of Computer Science & Engineering.

"Driving technical innovation through collaborative excellence and academic rigor for the engineers of tomorrow."

As an integral part of IERT, the society facilitates a bridge between the core curriculum and the rapidly evolving tech landscape. We focus on emerging domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain Technologies, and Cloud Infrastructure. Our members are encouraged to participate in national hackathons and departmental projects that contribute to the vision of a technically self-reliant India.
